Frequently Asked Questions

General

About Garabaly

Garabaly is West Africa's trusted livestock marketplace. It's a mobile app that connects livestock sellers with buyers — and the diaspora with home — with built-in escrow protection and real negotiation for every transaction. You can browse cattle, sheep, goats, camels, and poultry — with photos, verified seller profiles, and optional veterinary health certificates uploaded by sellers.

Garabaly is launching in Mali first and expanding across the Sahel and West Africa. Mobile money payments are already live in Mali, Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Burkina Faso, Benin, Togo, and Cameroun. Card payments are accepted from anywhere in the world — so diaspora buyers in Paris, London, New York, Brussels, Montreal, and beyond can use Garabaly today.

Garabaly is completely free for buyers — no platform fees, no commissions, no hidden charges. The price you see is the price you pay. A small platform fee is charged to sellers and service providers upon successful transactions only.

Full interface in French, English, and Arabic with native right-to-left (RTL) layout support — Arabic isn't an afterthought, it's a first-class experience. Voice descriptions and reviews can be recorded in any language: Bambara, Mooré, Hausa, Fulfulde, Wolof, Songhai, and more. In West Africa, voice is how business gets done — sellers describe their livestock by speaking, buyers leave voice reviews, and everyone communicates in their own language. No typing required.

Garabaly is purpose-built for West African livestock trade. Unlike generic marketplaces, we have escrow protection on every transaction, a dedicated negotiation feature (because "add to cart" doesn't exist in a Sahelian market), the ability for sellers to attach veterinary health certificates and for buyers to request vet inspections through our service provider network, voice descriptions in local languages, and special support for the diaspora to buy livestock for family back home.

Security & Trust

Safety & Security

Absolutely. All escrow funds are held in secure, regulated accounts — your payment is never mixed with Garabaly's operating funds. We use bank-grade encryption, industry-leading security standards for data integrity, and comprehensive monitoring. Your money is protected by architecture, not promises.

Card payments are processed by Stripe; we never store full card numbers on our servers. Sensitive payment data is handled with industry-standard protections end-to-end.

Every Garabaly order generates a unique 6-character alphanumeric confirmation code visible only to the buyer. When the livestock (or service) is delivered, the buyer enters this code directly in the app. Entering the code pays service providers immediately and starts the seller's 24-hour buyer-protection window (during which the buyer can open a dispute). The code is never shared with the seller or anyone else — only the buyer can enter it. This system prevents fraud and keeps everyone aligned on what "delivered" means.

Sellers and service providers pre-approve your order before you can checkout, so the order is already committed when you pay. After delivery, you have a 24-hour buyer protection window to open a dispute if something is wrong. If you don't confirm or dispute within that window, the seller's share is released automatically. This protection runs on our server-side scheduler — no action required from you unless you need to dispute.

If a buyer and seller can't agree, either party can open a dispute through the app. Garabaly's support team reviews the evidence (photos, messages, listing details, chat history). Disputes require dual-admin approval for resolution — meaning no single person can make a unilateral decision. During the dispute, funds remain in escrow. We aim to resolve all disputes quickly and fairly.

Your Garabaly wallet is protected by a private PIN that you set during onboarding. Your PIN is encrypted with industry-leading standards (never stored in plain text) and required for all sensitive operations like withdrawals. For additional security, we use SMS verification for high-value transactions, built-in fraud prevention to detect suspicious activity, and device verification to prevent unauthorized access.

You can report any listing or user directly in the app by tapping the report button. You can also reach our support team through the Contact page or via our social media channels. We take all reports seriously and investigate promptly. Our fraud detection system includes velocity limits and comprehensive event logging to maintain marketplace integrity.

Every transaction is protected by bank-grade encryption, military-grade integrity verification, encrypted confirmation codes, dispute mediation with manual approval, and built-in fraud prevention. This isn't a promise — it's our architecture.

Garabaly does not routinely read your messages. However, if a user files a report, our designated moderation staff may review the conversation to investigate. Deleted messages remain visible to moderators for evidence purposes and are retained for 90 days after deletion. This protects both buyers and sellers in disputes.

Tips for safe trade: inspect animals before paying, use our secure payment when possible, and never share financial PINs or passwords. For your safety, keep conversations on Garabaly — it lets us help if something goes wrong.

Services & Orders

Service Providers & Order Types

Garabaly supports three types of service providers: delivery drivers for livestock transport, professional butchers for meat preparation, and veterinarians for health inspections and certificates. Each service provider is verified, rated, and reviewed by previous clients. All service providers are paid through the same escrow system, so everyone is protected.

Garabaly supports four order pathways. By default, all items in your cart are independent orders — each with its own order ID and confirmation code. You can also place Seller-Only orders (just livestock), Service-Only orders (just a butcher, vet, or delivery), or Multiparty Bundle orders (combine livestock and services into one order). In bundles, you drag-to-reorder the fulfillment sequence — full autonomy over which services come after the seller.

Every order has a real-time progress tracker visible in the app. You'll see each stage — from order placed, to seller confirmed, preparation, in transit, delivered, and completed. Push notifications and in-app alerts keep you updated at every stage. For multiparty orders, you'll see the progress of each party (seller, delivery, butcher) independently.

Yes! You can create a Service-Only order to book a butcher, delivery driver, or vet independently. This is useful if you've already purchased your livestock elsewhere but need a professional service. The service provider is still paid through Garabaly's escrow for your protection.
