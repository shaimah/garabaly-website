    General

    What is Garabaly?

    Garabaly is the trusted livestock marketplace. It's a mobile app that connects livestock sellers with buyers — and the diaspora with home — with built-in escrow protection and real negotiation for every transaction. You can browse cattle, sheep, goats, camels, and poultry — with photos, verified seller profiles, and optional veterinary health certificates uploaded by sellers.

    Where is Garabaly available?

    Garabaly is launching in Mali first and expanding across the Sahel and West Africa. Mobile money payments are already live in Mali, Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Burkina Faso, Benin, Togo, and Cameroun. Card payments are accepted from anywhere in the world — so diaspora buyers in Paris, London, New York, Brussels, Montreal, and beyond can use Garabaly today.

    Is the app free to download?

    Garabaly is completely free for buyers — no platform fees, no commissions, no hidden charges. The price you see is the price you pay. A small platform fee is charged to sellers and service providers upon successful transactions only.

    What languages does Garabaly support?

    Full interface in French, English, and Arabic with native right-to-left (RTL) layout support — Arabic isn't an afterthought, it's a first-class experience. Voice descriptions and reviews can be recorded in any language: Bambara, Mooré, Hausa, Fulfulde, Wolof, Songhai, and more. Voice is how business gets done — sellers describe their livestock by speaking, buyers leave voice reviews, and everyone communicates in their own language. No typing required.

    How is Garabaly different from other marketplaces?

    Garabaly is purpose-built for the livestock trade. Unlike generic marketplaces, we have escrow protection on every transaction, a dedicated negotiation feature (because "add to cart" doesn't exist in a Sahelian market), the ability for sellers to attach veterinary health certificates and for buyers to request vet inspections through our service provider network, voice descriptions in local languages, and special support for the diaspora to buy livestock for family back home.

    Can I chat with sellers using voice messages?

    Yes! Garabaly's chat supports text messages, voice recordings, photos, and video. You can also share listing details directly in the chat. Voice messages are especially popular — they let sellers describe their livestock in any language and let buyers ask detailed questions naturally. Messages are delivered in real time with offline queuing so nothing gets lost.

    What are "Pioneers" and "Expo Go"?

    While Garabaly is in review on Google Play and the App Store, our earliest users — the Pioneers — get the app through Expo Go, a tool that runs Garabaly on your phone from a QR code or a link. It's intentional, exclusive early access — and entirely real: your account, your wallet, your orders are all live and stay with you when the app launches in the stores.

    How do I get started?

    1. Visit garabaly.com and tap the Download button — or go straight to the Get the App page.
    2. Install Expo Go on your phone (we provide QR codes and direct links for the App Store and Google Play).
    3. Scan the Garabaly QR code — with your phone's camera on iPhone, or with the Expo Go app on Android — or tap the direct "Open in Expo Go" link. Both work on both platforms.
    4. Garabaly opens inside Expo Go. Create your account as you normally would.

    Will my Expo Go account survive when the app launches on the stores?

    Yes. The Expo Go version and the store version are exactly the same product on the same servers. Your account, your wallet, your orders, your reputation score, your reviews — everything migrates with no action from you. Just download the store version when it launches and log in with the same credentials.

    When will the app launch officially on the stores?

    The decision belongs to Apple and Google. The app was submitted in April 2026 and review is in progress. As soon as we have news, we share it on Facebook, TikTok and WhatsApp. While you wait, your Expo Go access is fully functional.

    I found a bug — where do I send it?

Send it on WhatsApp +223 62 77 88 71 or by email at support@garabaly.com: the screen where it happened (screenshot or video if possible), what you were doing right before, and your phone model / OS version. We'll keep you posted on the fix.

    Ordering & negotiating

    How does the order process work?

    When you add items to your cart, the seller (and any service providers) must first confirm availability before you can checkout. This pre-checkout approval protects all parties. Once confirmed, you pay and your funds are held securely in escrow until delivery is complete.

    How does negotiation work on Garabaly?

    Garabaly has a dedicated negotiation feature built in. You can chat directly with sellers, ask for more photos or videos, negotiate the price, and reach consensus — just like at a physical market. Once you and the seller agree on a price, you proceed to payment with escrow protection. We believe price is a conversation, not a label.

    Does Garabaly charge buyers any fees?

    No. Garabaly is completely free for buyers. There are no platform fees, no commissions, and no hidden charges. The price you see is the price you pay. We only charge a small platform fee to sellers and service providers upon successful transactions.

    How does escrow protection work?

    Garabaly uses per-party escrow. When you pay, your money is held securely. Nothing is released until you enter your 6-character confirmation code at delivery. The moment you enter it, the service providers (butcher, vet, transporter) are paid and the seller's share enters a 24-hour buyer-protection window — during those 24 hours, you can open a dispute if anything is wrong. Otherwise, the seller's share is released automatically at the end of the window — you don't have to do anything. If a dispute is opened, all unreleased shares are held and our team (two people approve together) makes the call.

    What if the animal is sick or different from the photos?

    You're protected. After delivery, inspect the animal carefully before you enter your confirmation code. Verify that it matches the listing — including photos, description, and any health documentation provided by the seller. If something is wrong, open a dispute directly in the app before confirming. Garabaly's team reviews the evidence and mediates. If the dispute is resolved in your favor, you receive a full refund from escrow. Your money is never released until you're satisfied.

    How long do I have to inspect the livestock?

    Once the livestock is delivered, you have a reasonable inspection period to verify that it matches the listing. The specific timeframe is shown in your escrow details within the app. If you don't confirm or dispute within the allotted period, the escrow is automatically released to the seller.

    What service providers are available on Garabaly?

    Garabaly supports three types of service providers: delivery drivers for livestock transport, professional butchers for meat preparation, and veterinarians for health inspections and certificates. Each service provider is verified, rated, and reviewed by previous clients. All service providers are paid through the same escrow system, so everyone is protected.

    What are the different order types?

    By default, every item in your cart is an independent order — each with its own order ID and confirmation code, whether it is an animal or a standalone service like a butcher, vet, or delivery. When you want everything under one code, you can bundle one listing with up to 3 services (maximum 1 delivery + 1 butcher + 1 vet) into a single order, and drag-to-reorder the fulfillment sequence. So there are really two ways to order: independent by default, or bundled when you choose.

    How does order tracking work?

    Every order has a real-time progress tracker visible in the app. You'll see each stage — from order placed, to seller confirmed, preparation, in transit, delivered, and completed. Push notifications and in-app alerts keep you updated at every stage. For multiparty orders, you'll see the progress of each party (seller, delivery, butcher) independently.

    Can I book a butcher without buying livestock on the app?

    Yes! You can book a butcher, delivery driver, or vet on its own — it is simply an independent order, with no animal purchase required. This is useful if you've already purchased your livestock elsewhere but need a professional service. The service provider is still paid through Garabaly's escrow for your protection.

    Payments & diaspora

    Can I buy livestock for my family from abroad?

    Absolutely! Garabaly is designed for the diaspora. Whether you're in Paris, London, New York, Washington DC, Brussels, Montreal, or anywhere else, you can browse livestock, negotiate with local sellers, pay with escrow protection, and have the animal delivered to your family's compound. It's perfect for Tabaski, weddings, naming ceremonies (baptêmes), funerals, or simply caring for your loved ones from afar.

    How do I pay for livestock?

    Garabaly supports multiple payment methods:

    • Mobile Money (LIVE): Pay with Orange Money, Moov Money, Wave, with more providers being added. Available in Mali, Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Burkina Faso, Benin, Togo, and Cameroun.
    • Card Payments (LIVE): Visa, Mastercard via Stripe. You pay in your own currency at a rate fixed when you pay — no conversion fee within the CFA zone or in euros, and a small fee shown upfront for other currencies.
    • Garabaly Wallet (LIVE): Deposit via card or mobile money, instant wallet-to-wallet transfers.
    • Cash: pay the seller directly on delivery — no escrow hold and limited protection. We recommend an escrow-backed method (card, mobile money, or wallet) for full safety.

    Which mobile money providers work with Garabaly?

    Garabaly supports Orange Money, Moov Money, and Wave. Mobile money is available in Mali, Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Burkina Faso, Benin, Togo, and Cameroun. New providers and countries are being added regularly.

    What's the difference between cash on delivery and escrow?

    With escrow-backed methods (card, mobile money, wallet), your funds are held securely until you confirm delivery with your confirmation code. After you enter the code, the seller's share enters a 24-hour buyer-protection window — during those 24 hours you can open a dispute if anything is wrong. With cash on delivery, you pay the seller directly when the livestock arrives. Cash offers limited platform protection — we recommend escrow-backed payment methods for maximum safety.

    Can my family receive the livestock without downloading the app?

    Yes. Your family doesn't need to download Garabaly. You place the order, pay, and track delivery from wherever you are. The livestock is delivered directly to your family's compound. You can coordinate the delivery time so you're on a WhatsApp call when it arrives — many diaspora buyers do exactly that.

    What exchange rate do you apply for the diaspora?

    EUR ↔ XOF: fixed at 655.957 (the official CFA peg), with no Garabaly markup. Other currencies (USD, GBP, CAD…) use the Stripe rate at the moment of payment; you always see the XOF amount the seller will receive before you pay.

    My mobile money payment failed — is it you or my operator?

    It's almost always a refusal on the operator side (insufficient balance, USSD prompt not confirmed, daily cap reached, brief outage). Check your balance via the operator USSD, check no cap has been hit, and try again in the app. If it fails again, change method (another operator or card). If you were debited but Garabaly didn't confirm, message us immediately on WhatsApp +223 62 77 88 71 — that's a top priority, response within a few hours, day or night.

    When should I order for Tabaski / Eid al-Adha?

    For a stress-free Eid (T = day of the celebration):

    • T - 4 weeks: pick your seller, negotiate.
    • T - 3 weeks: pay and lock your livestock.
    • T - 1 week: coordinate delivery with your family.
    • T - 3 to T - 1 day: delivery to the family compound.
    • Day T: confirm by call + enter your code.

    Eid week, roads are loaded, prices spike, and transporters are saturated. Ordering at T - 3 weeks is cheaper, calmer, and more reliable.

    The date of Tabaski (Eid al-Adha) shifts by ~11 days each year based on moon sighting. Check the official calendar for the current year's exact date.

    Can I see when my gift arrives?

    Absolutely. Many diaspora buyers coordinate the delivery timing so they can be on a WhatsApp call when their family receives the livestock. Sellers' WhatsApp contact information is available in their profile. It's become one of the most emotional parts of using Garabaly — seeing your mother's joy from thousands of kilometers away.

    What currencies does Garabaly support?

    You always pay in your own currency, and the seller is always paid in theirs — Garabaly handles the conversion. The rate is fixed the moment you pay and shown before you confirm; the same fixed rate is used for any refund. Within the CFA franc zone (XOF/XAF) and for euros, payment is at the official fixed rate with no conversion fee. For other currencies — such as USD, GBP, CAD — a small conversion fee applies and is always shown upfront. Cross-currency payment is currently available by card and Garabaly Wallet.

    I'm in the diaspora — how do I pay from abroad?

    You pay in your own currency, and the seller is paid in theirs — Garabaly handles the conversion in the middle. The rate is fixed the moment you pay and shown to you before you confirm, and the same fixed rate is used if you are ever refunded. If you pay in euros, we use the official fixed rate (1 € = 655.957 FCFA) with no conversion fee. For other currencies a small conversion fee applies, always shown upfront. Paying from abroad is available by card and Garabaly Wallet.

    How do I get verified as a seller?

    To become a verified seller, download the app and create an account. Then complete the verification process by providing a valid ID and a selfie. Verified sellers earn a badge on their profile, which builds trust with buyers and increases the visibility of your listings.

    When do I receive payment?

    Nothing is released until the buyer enters their 6-character confirmation code. The moment they do, the service providers (butchers, veterinarians, delivery drivers) are paid and your seller's share enters a 24-hour buyer-protection window — the window applies only to the seller's share, and there is no per-step payout before the code. If no dispute is raised during that window, the funds are released automatically to your Garabaly Wallet — you don't have to do anything. Once released, funds are available in your Garabaly Wallet for instant transfer to other users or external withdrawal via Orange Money, Moov Money, or Wave, with more providers being added. Bank withdrawal is also available.

    How do I withdraw my earnings?

    Your Garabaly Wallet supports deposits via card or mobile money, escrow releases, and instant wallet-to-wallet transfers to other users. Withdraw via Orange Money, Moov Money, Wave, with more providers being added. Bank withdrawal is also available. All wallet operations require your secure PIN (set during onboarding). Your wallet uses auditable financial records and tamper-proof balance checks to ensure every transaction is accurate.

    What fees does Garabaly charge sellers?

    Garabaly charges a small platform fee to sellers upon successful sale, and to service providers upon task completion. No upfront costs, no listing fees. Buyers always pay zero fees. 0% for buyers, always. Sellers pay 5% — and only ever less — charged only once you've been paid.

    Why can't I withdraw on my first day?

    For your safety, withdrawals are blocked during the first 24 hours after account creation. This is a protection against account takeover right after sign-up. Everything else — browse, negotiate, pay, sell, receive payments into your wallet — works normally during this window. Only outgoing withdrawal waits for the period to end.

    A lot. Verification multiplies your limits by roughly 20× — you go from 200 000 XOF to 5 000 000 XOF per day for withdrawals. You also get a verification badge on your profile (which builds buyer trust and lifts your listing visibility), unlock professional-seller features (unlimited listings, larger transactions), and become eligible for Trusted status after 90 days of clean trading.

    Security & trust

    Is my money safe with Garabaly?

    Absolutely. All escrow funds are held in secure, regulated accounts — your payment is never mixed with Garabaly's operating funds. We use strong encryption, strict data-integrity safeguards, and comprehensive monitoring. Your money is protected by architecture, not promises.

    Card payments are processed by Stripe; we never store full card numbers on our servers. Sensitive payment data is handled with industry-standard protections end-to-end.

    What is the confirmation code and how does it work?

    Every Garabaly order generates a unique 6-character alphanumeric confirmation code visible only to the buyer. When the livestock (or service) is delivered, the buyer enters this code directly in the app. Nothing is released before that: entering the code pays the service providers and starts the seller's 24-hour buyer-protection window (during which the buyer can open a dispute). One code releases the whole order — independent or bundled — with no per-step payout. The code is never shared with the seller or anyone else — only the buyer can enter it. This system prevents fraud and keeps everyone aligned on what "delivered" means.

    What happens if the seller doesn't confirm my order?

    Sellers and service providers pre-approve your order before you can checkout, so the order is already committed when you pay. After delivery, you have a 24-hour buyer protection window to open a dispute if something is wrong. If you don't confirm or dispute within that window, the seller's share is released automatically. This protection runs on our server-side scheduler — no action required from you unless you need to dispute.

    What happens if there's a dispute?

    If a buyer and seller can't agree, either party can open a dispute through the app. Garabaly's support team reviews the evidence (photos, messages, listing details, chat history). Disputes require dual-admin approval for resolution — meaning no single person can make a unilateral decision. During the dispute, funds remain in escrow. We aim to resolve all disputes quickly and fairly.

    How is my wallet secured?

    Your Garabaly wallet is protected by a private PIN that you set during onboarding. Your PIN is encrypted with strong standards (never stored in plain text) and required for all sensitive operations like withdrawals. For additional security, we use SMS verification for high-value transactions, built-in fraud prevention to detect suspicious activity, and device verification to prevent unauthorized access.

    How do I report a suspicious listing or user?

    You can report any listing or user directly in the app by tapping the report button. You can also reach our support team through the Contact page or via our social media channels. We take all reports seriously and investigate promptly. Our fraud detection system includes velocity limits and comprehensive event logging to maintain marketplace integrity.

    How secure is the escrow system really?

    Every transaction is protected by strong encryption, tamper-proof integrity checks, encrypted confirmation codes, dispute mediation with manual approval, and built-in fraud prevention. This isn't a promise — it's our architecture.

    Can Garabaly read my messages?

    Garabaly does not routinely read your messages. However, if a user files a report, our designated moderation staff may review the conversation to investigate. Deleted messages remain visible to moderators for evidence purposes — for example, to resolve a dispute — for as long as your account exists, and are removed when you delete your account. This protects both buyers and sellers in disputes.

    Tips for safe trade: inspect animals before paying, use our secure payment when possible, and never share financial PINs or passwords. For your safety, keep conversations on Garabaly — it lets us help if something goes wrong.

    Account & data

    How do I delete my account?

    Go to Profile → Settings → Delete my account. The app asks for your PIN as a safeguard. If you have funds in your wallet or open orders, you must withdraw or finalize them first. Confirm: your account is marked for deletion and anonymized within 30 days. You can also email support@garabaly.com with the subject "Account deletion request" if you can't do it from the app.

    What is kept: financial transaction records (invoices, accounting ledgers), anonymized with respect to your identity — to satisfy legal obligations.

    How do I get a copy of my data?

    You have the right to a copy of your data. Email us at support@garabaly.com from the email on your account (or from a verified phone number) with the subject "Data access request — [your name]". We send back an archive (JSON + readable PDF) within 30 days maximum, usually within 7-10 days.

    What is the minimum age to use Garabaly?

    You must be at least 18 — the age of legal majority in our markets — to use Garabaly. Garabaly is a marketplace for buying and selling livestock and services, and using it means entering binding purchase agreements and making payments, which require legal capacity. By creating an account you confirm you meet this requirement (see our Terms of Service). If we find an account belongs to a minor, we close it and return any funds to a legal guardian after verification.
