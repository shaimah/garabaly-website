Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using Garabaly for buying and selling livestock.

General

About Garabaly

Garabaly is West Africa's trusted livestock marketplace. It's a mobile app that connects livestock sellers with buyers — and the diaspora with home — with built-in escrow protection and real negotiation for every transaction. You can browse cattle, sheep, goats, and poultry — all verified with photos, seller profiles, and health certificates.

Garabaly is available across West Africa and is expanding rapidly through the Sahel region. We serve buyers and sellers in multiple countries, with plans to grow into Senegal, Burkina Faso, Niger, and more. The diaspora can use Garabaly from anywhere in the world — Paris, London, New York, Washington DC, Brussels, Montreal, and beyond.

Garabaly is completely free for buyers — no platform fees, no commissions, no hidden charges. The price you see is the price you pay. A small platform fee is charged to sellers and service providers upon successful transactions only.

Full interface in French, English, and Arabic with native right-to-left (RTL) layout support — Arabic isn't an afterthought, it's a first-class experience. Voice descriptions and reviews can be recorded in any language: Bambara, Mooré, Hausa, Fulfulde, Wolof, Songhai, and more. We're adding Bambara, Mooré, Hausa, Fulfulde, and Wolof as full interface languages based on user demand. Total estimated: 1,300+ translatable strings, all human-reviewed — not machine translated.

Garabaly is purpose-built for West African livestock trade. Unlike generic marketplaces, we have escrow protection on every transaction, a dedicated negotiation feature (because "add to cart" doesn't exist in a Sahelian market), veterinary health certificates, voice descriptions in local languages, and special support for the diaspora to buy livestock for family back home.

Selling

For Sellers

To become a verified seller, download the app and create an account. Then complete the verification process by providing a valid ID and confirming your location. Verified sellers earn a badge on their profile, which builds trust with buyers and increases the visibility of your listings.

When the buyer enters the 6-character confirmation code, your payment enters a 24-hour buyer protection window. If no dispute is raised during that period, funds are automatically released to your Garabaly Wallet. Once released, funds are available in your Garabaly Wallet. You can transfer to other users instantly. Your wallet uses double-entry bookkeeping and SHA-256 checksums to ensure every transaction is accurate.

Security & Trust

Safety & Security

Absolutely. All escrow funds are held in secure, regulated accounts with atomic database transactions — your payment is never mixed with Garabaly's operating funds. We use bank-grade encryption, bcrypt-hashed PINs, SHA-256 checksums for data integrity, and comprehensive security event logging. Our payment architecture has passed a world-class security audit.

Every Garabaly order generates a unique 6-character alphanumeric confirmation code visible only to the buyer. When the livestock (or service) is delivered, the buyer enters this code directly in the app to confirm receipt and trigger the escrow release. The code is never shared with the seller or anyone else — only the buyer can enter it. This system prevents fraud and ensures both parties are protected.

Sellers and service providers pre-approve your order before you can checkout, so the order is already committed when you pay. After delivery, you have a 24-hour buyer protection window to open a dispute if something is wrong. If you don't confirm or dispute within that window, the seller's share is released automatically. This protection runs on our server-side scheduler — no action required from you unless you need to dispute.

If a buyer and seller can't agree, either party can open a dispute through the app. Garabaly's support team reviews the evidence (photos, messages, listing details, chat history). Disputes require dual-admin approval for resolution — meaning no single person can make a unilateral decision. During the dispute, funds remain in escrow. We aim to resolve all disputes quickly and fairly.

Your Garabaly wallet is protected by a private PIN that you set during onboarding. The PIN is bcrypt-hashed (never stored in plain text) and required for all sensitive operations like withdrawals. For additional security, we use SMS OTP verification for high-value transactions, velocity limits to prevent rapid suspicious activity, and device fingerprinting to detect unauthorized access.

You can report any listing or user directly in the app by tapping the report button. You can also reach our support team through the Contact page or via our social media channels. We take all reports seriously and investigate promptly. Our fraud detection system includes velocity limits and comprehensive event logging to maintain marketplace integrity.

Our escrow system passed a comprehensive security audit with a 95% compliance score. Technical details: atomic transactions with PostgreSQL row-level locking, SHA-256 checksums for every transaction, bcrypt-hashed confirmation codes (cost factor 10), dual admin approval for dispute resolution, and velocity limits to prevent fraud. This isn't a promise — it's our architecture.

Services & Orders

Service Providers & Order Types

Garabaly supports three types of service providers: delivery drivers for livestock transport, professional butchers for meat preparation, and veterinarians for health inspections and certificates. Each service provider is verified, rated, and reviewed by previous clients. All service providers are paid through the same escrow system, so everyone is protected.

Garabaly supports four order pathways. By default, all items in your cart are independent orders — each with its own order ID and confirmation code. You can also place Seller-Only orders (just livestock), Service-Only orders (just a butcher, vet, or delivery), or Multiparty Bundle orders (combine livestock and services into one order). In bundles, you drag-to-reorder the fulfillment sequence — full autonomy over which services come after the seller.

Every order has a real-time progress tracker visible in the app. You'll see each stage — from order placed, to seller confirmed, preparation, in transit, delivered, and completed. Push notifications and in-app alerts keep you updated at every stage. For multiparty orders, you'll see the progress of each party (seller, delivery, butcher) independently.

Yes! You can create a Service-Only order to book a butcher, delivery driver, or vet independently. This is useful if you've already purchased your livestock elsewhere but need a professional service. The service provider is still paid through Garabaly's escrow for your protection.
