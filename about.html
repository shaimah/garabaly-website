Our Story

Everyone said digitizing West African livestock trade was impossible. The markets are too informal. The herders don't use apps. The trust can't be replicated. They were wrong.

Our Mission

The Trust Layer
That Never Existed

Livestock is the backbone of West Africa's economy and culture. Millions of families depend on it for their livelihood, and millions more rely on it for celebrations — Tabaski, weddings, naming ceremonies, and everyday meals. Yet every transaction has always required a leap of faith.

Garabaly is building the trust layer that Africa's livestock trade never had — a marketplace where every payment is escrow-protected, every negotiation happens as a conversation, and every trader's reputation follows them from the market to the phone and back.

The Problem

Why Traditional Livestock
Trading Falls Short

In West Africa's livestock trade, buyers often need to travel hundreds of kilometers to inspect animals. Cash transactions leave no protection against fraud. Middlemen add costs without adding value. And there's no reliable way to verify an animal's health before purchase.

Meanwhile, the diaspora — millions of West Africans living abroad — have no way to buy livestock for their families back home for celebrations. These inefficiencies cost farmers their livelihoods and cost buyers their hard-earned money. Garabaly exists to change this.

Our Approach

"Add to Cart" Doesn't Exist
in a Sahelian Market

Every transaction in West Africa is a relationship. You greet. You ask about the family. You inspect. You offer. They counter. You agree. That's not friction — that's culture.

Garabaly doesn't skip the negotiation. We digitized it. Because you can't digitize a marketplace without respecting how it actually works. Trust isn't built with a checkout button.

In West Africa, around two-thirds of livestock crosses at least one border before reaching its final buyer. This isn't e-commerce. It's relationships spanning thousands of kilometers. We built for that reality.

The Founder

Moctar Kané

Herder Butcher Buyer Diaspora

I grew up in rural Mali. Not in Bamako — in the villages. Kenenkoun. Bouramabougou. Every week, I accompanied families to the weekly market. I lived with herders. I helped maintain their herds. I saw what it costs to walk for days to reach a market fair — and I know the fear of the journey home, when you've sold and all your money is in your pocket, on a road where everyone knows it.

When I was in school, I worked as a butcher to make a living. I know the weight of a knife, the precision of the cut, the difference between clean work and sloppy work. I know that during Tabaski, a butcher works from sunrise to sunset and every family depends on him. And I also know that the rest of the year, that same butcher waits — because there's no way to be known beyond his neighbourhood.

I was also sent to find livestock — long trips, across multiple markets, looking for the right animal at the right price. I've known the wait under the sun, the markets where nothing is good enough, coming back empty-handed, and the relief when you finally find one and the price holds.

Today, I live in the diaspora. And like millions of others, when Tabaski comes, when there's a baptism, a wedding, a funeral — I want to send a ram to my family. From far away. Without being there. Calling three times to ask: "Is it done? Was it a good one?"

I've been a herder. I've been a butcher. I've been a buyer. I am diaspora.

Garabaly wasn't born in an office. It was born from all of this.

In March 2026, I registered Garabaly in Bamako at Mali's Trade and Movable Credit Register (RCCM). With that registration, we integrated mobile money services and built the foundations of a real company, by the book.

My commitment is simple: every feature on Garabaly is tested by real sellers in real markets. Every CFA franc is protected by escrow. And every decision is made by someone who has lived on both sides of the market.

Built here, for here.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Every decision we make is guided by these principles.

Trust First

Every feature we build starts with the question: "Does this make the transaction more trustworthy?" Our escrow system is the foundation.

Locally Rooted, Globally Connected

Full interface in French, English, and Arabic with native RTL support — not an afterthought. Specialized livestock vocabulary: Bovin, Ovin, Caprin. Payment terms in local context: 'Paiement sécurisé (Escrow)'. Voice descriptions and reviews in any language — Bambara, Mooré, Hausa, Fulfulde, Wolof, and more. Because in West Africa, voice is how business gets done.

Security You Can Verify

Escrow that holds your money safely until you confirm. Every payment is checked and recorded, so nothing slips through. Your PIN and confirmation code are encrypted and never shared. Dispute mediation with manual approval. Built-in fraud prevention and brute-force protection. Nothing is released until you enter your code — then the service providers are paid and the seller's share enters a 24-hour buyer-dispute window. This isn't a promise — it's our architecture.

Mobile-First Design

Most of our users access Garabaly from their smartphones. We design for mobile first, with low bandwidth usage and intuitive interfaces.

Animal Welfare

We partner with veterinarians to encourage health certification. Healthy livestock benefits everyone in the supply chain.

Financial Inclusion

A full digital wallet with auditable financial records, escrow balances, card deposits, and wallet-to-wallet transfers. Mobile money payments via Orange Money, Moov Money, Wave among several others — LIVE. Bank withdrawal is also available. Bringing millions of traders into the digital economy.

Built to Scale

Technology That Earns Trust

Behind Garabaly's simple interface is infrastructure built for security, speed, and scale.

Encrypted Escrow Engine

Per-party staged escrow that protects every payment, keeps an exact record of every franc, and releases money only when the deal is done. One escrow hold, per-party allocation. Seller's share is held for a 24-hour dispute window after buyer confirmation. Service providers receive immediate payment upon completion. Every CFA franc is accounted for with mathematical precision.

Real-Time Communication

Real-time chat with offline queuing, read receipts, typing indicators, voice messages, and shared listing cards. Communication that works even on low bandwidth.

Smart Notifications

Dual delivery system with push notifications and in-app alerts. Deep linking takes you directly to orders, chats, or wallet — never miss an important update.

Fraud Prevention

Built-in fraud prevention, device verification, encrypted PINs, SMS verification, and comprehensive security monitoring. Protection at every layer.
