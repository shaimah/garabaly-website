Our Story

Everyone said digitizing West African livestock trade was impossible. The markets are too informal. The herders don't use apps. The trust can't be replicated. They were wrong.

Our Mission

Making Livestock Trade
Safe & Accessible

Livestock is the backbone of West Africa's economy and culture. Millions of families depend on it for their livelihood, and millions more rely on it for celebrations like Tabaski.

Yet the industry has been plagued by fraud, lack of transparency, and the difficulty of trading across long distances. Garabaly was born to solve these problems — by creating a marketplace where trust is built into every transaction through our escrow protection system, and where negotiation happens the way it always has: as a conversation.

The Problem

Why Traditional Livestock
Trading Falls Short

In West Africa's livestock trade, buyers often need to travel hundreds of kilometers to inspect animals. Cash transactions leave no protection against fraud. Middlemen add costs without adding value. And there's no reliable way to verify an animal's health before purchase.

Meanwhile, the diaspora — millions of West Africans living abroad — have no way to buy livestock for their families back home for celebrations. These inefficiencies cost farmers their livelihoods and cost buyers their hard-earned money. Garabaly exists to change this.

Our Approach

"Add to Cart" Doesn't Exist
in a Sahelian Market

Every transaction in West Africa is a relationship. You greet. You ask about the family. You inspect. You offer. They counter. You agree. That's not friction — that's culture.

Garabaly doesn't skip the negotiation. We digitized it. Because you can't digitize a marketplace without respecting how it actually works. Trust isn't built with a checkout button.

In West Africa, around two-thirds of livestock crosses at least one border before reaching its final buyer. This isn't e-commerce. It's relationships spanning thousands of kilometers. We built for that reality.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Every decision we make is guided by these principles.

Trust First

Every feature we build starts with the question: "Does this make the transaction more trustworthy?" Our escrow system is the foundation.

Locally Rooted, Globally Connected

Full interface in French, English, and Arabic with native RTL support — not an afterthought. Specialized livestock vocabulary: Bovin, Ovin, Caprin. Payment terms in local context: 'Paiement sécurisé (Escrow)'. Voice descriptions in Bambara, Mooré, Hausa, Fulfulde, Wolof — any language. Coming soon: Bambara, Mooré, Hausa, Fulfulde, and Wolof as full app languages.

World-Class Security

Per-party staged escrow with atomic transactions. SHA-256 checksums for every transaction. Bcrypt-hashed PINs (cost factor 12). Dual admin approval for disputes. Velocity limits: 5 transactions/hour for new users. 30-minute lockout after 3 failed code attempts. Seller's share held for 24-hour buyer dispute window. Service providers paid immediately upon completion. This isn't marketing — it's our audit report.

Mobile-First Design

Most of our users access Garabaly from their smartphones. We design for mobile first, with low bandwidth usage and intuitive interfaces.

Animal Welfare

We partner with veterinarians to encourage health certification. Healthy livestock benefits everyone in the supply chain.

Financial Inclusion

A full digital wallet with double-entry bookkeeping, escrow balances, and instant withdrawals via Orange Money, Wave, MTN MoMo, and more. Bringing millions of traders into the digital economy.

For the Diaspora

Built to Scale

Technology That Earns Trust

Behind Garabaly's simple interface is a world-class infrastructure built for security, speed, and scale.

Encrypted Escrow Engine

Per-party staged escrow with atomic transactions, SHA-256 checksums, and double-entry bookkeeping. One escrow hold, per-party allocation. Seller's share is held for a 24-hour dispute window after buyer confirmation. Service providers receive immediate payment upon completion. Every CFA franc is accounted for with mathematical precision.

Real-Time Communication

Socket.io-powered chat with offline queuing, read receipts, typing indicators, voice messages, and shared listing cards. Communication that works even on low bandwidth.

Smart Notifications

Dual delivery system with push notifications and in-app alerts. Deep linking takes you directly to orders, chats, or wallet — never miss an important update.

Fraud Prevention

Velocity limits, device fingerprinting, bcrypt-hashed PINs, SMS OTP verification, and comprehensive security event logging. Protection at every layer.

Looking Ahead

Our Vision for the Future

We envision a West Africa where any herder, farmer, or livestock trader — regardless of their location or access to traditional financial services — can participate in a fair, transparent marketplace.

As we grow, we plan to expand across the entire Sahel region and beyond — Senegal, Burkina Faso, Niger, and more. Our roadmap includes adding Bambara, Mooré, Hausa, Fulfulde, and Wolof as full app languages, deepening mobile money integration with Orange Money, Wave, and MTN MoMo APIs, and building an admin dashboard with dual-approval oversight for complete transparency.

Our goal is to become the standard platform for livestock commerce in Africa while preserving the cultural traditions that make this trade unique.
