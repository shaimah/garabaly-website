Why Garabaly

Trust Isn't Built With a Checkout Button

Every transaction in West Africa is a relationship. You greet. You ask about the family. You inspect. You offer. They counter. You agree. That's not friction — that's culture. We digitized the negotiation. We didn't replace it.

Escrow Protection

Your money stays locked until the cow is in your compound. Period. Five-stage escrow lifecycle. SHA-256 checksums. Bcrypt-hashed confirmation codes. World-class security — not just words.

Real-Time Chat & Negotiation

Real-time messaging built in — text, voice messages, photos, and video. Negotiate prices directly, share listing details in-chat, and see read receipts. Price is a conversation, not a label.

Multi-Currency & Diaspora

Pay from Paris in euros. Pay from New York in dollars. Your family in Bamako receives the ram in CFA. 54 African currencies. Fixed XOF-EUR rate (655.957). Real-time conversion for everything else.

Real-Time Order Tracking

Track your order through every stage — from confirmation to preparation, delivery, and receipt. Live status updates via push notifications and in-app alerts. Always know where your livestock is.

Voice Descriptions & Reviews

Mali's literacy rate is 31%. Voice isn't a feature — it's a necessity. Sellers describe their livestock the way they always have: by speaking. Buyers leave voice reviews. No typing required. Communication without barriers.

Digital Wallet & Mobile Money

A full-featured wallet with secure PIN protection, transaction history, and instant withdrawals. Pay and get paid via Orange Money, Wave, MTN MoMo, Moov Money, or bank transfer.

Service Provider Network

Book verified butchers, delivery drivers, and veterinarians directly in the app. Each service provider has ratings, reviews, and guaranteed payment through the same escrow system.

Multilingual App

Full interface in French, English, and Arabic (with right-to-left support). Specialized livestock vocabulary and locale-aware formatting. Bambara, Mooré, Hausa, Fulfulde, and Wolof coming soon.

Your Money is Safe

Escrow Protection
for Every Transaction

In traditional livestock trading, sending money to a stranger is a leap of faith. Garabaly eliminates that risk with a five-stage escrow lifecycle, encrypted transactions, and fraud detection.

Confirmation Code System

Every delivery is verified with a unique 6-character code. Only the buyer can release funds — ensuring you pay only for what you receive.

24-Hour Countdown Protection

Sellers must confirm orders within 24 hours, or the transaction is automatically cancelled and your funds are returned instantly.

Fraud Detection & Security

Velocity limits, device fingerprinting, and real-time monitoring protect every transaction. Your wallet is secured with a private PIN and SMS verification.

Full Transparency

Track every transaction stage in real time — from escrow hold to fund release. Double-entry bookkeeping with SHA-256 checksums ensures your records are always accurate.
