Your Money is Safe

Escrow Protection
for Every Transaction

In traditional livestock trading, sending money to a stranger is a leap of faith. Garabaly eliminates that risk with per-party staged escrow, encrypted transactions, and fraud detection. One escrow hold, per-party allocation — each provider gets paid as their step is completed.

Have questions about how escrow works? -> See our FAQ

Confirmation Code System

One 6-character code per order — even multiparty bundles. Encrypted with industry-leading security standards and protected by built-in brute-force prevention. For escrow orders, the code is hidden from seller and providers until buyer confirms receipt. One confirmation completes the order and all service bookings simultaneously.

24-Hour Buyer Protection Window

After you receive the livestock and confirm with your code, the seller's payment is held for 24 hours. During this window, you can open a dispute if something is wrong. Service providers (butcher, vet, delivery) get paid immediately — the 24-hour window applies only to the seller's share.

Fraud Detection & Security

Velocity limits, device fingerprinting, and real-time monitoring protect every transaction. Your wallet is secured with a private PIN and SMS verification.

Full Transparency

Per-party escrow tracking visible to all parties — buyer, seller, and providers. Real-time status updates delivered instantly. Escrow countdown timer visible during the 24-hour dispute window. Payment-method-aware labels for cash vs escrow orders. Auditable financial records ensure every CFA franc is accounted for.
