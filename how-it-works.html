Order Model

Independent by default — bundle when you want

Every cart item is its own order with its own code and tracker. Or group one listing with up to 3 services into a single order — one code, one tracker.

Independent Default

Every cart item — an animal or a service — is a separate order with its own ORD- id, its own code, its own tracker.

  • 1 animal = 1 order
  • 1 service = 1 order

Bundle Optional

Group one seller listing + up to 3 services (max 1 delivery + 1 butcher + 1 vet) into one order, one code, one tracker.

  • Delivery · Butcher · Vet
  • 1 animal + up to 3 services (1 of each)

You choose at cart — bundle or keep separate. Always escrow-protected.
