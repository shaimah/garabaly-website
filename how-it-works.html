How Garabaly Works

A step-by-step guide to buying and selling livestock with negotiation and escrow protection.

For Buyers

How to Buy Livestock on Garabaly

Find the perfect livestock, negotiate directly, pay securely, and receive with confidence — all from your phone, anywhere in the world.

1

Download & Sign Up

Get the Garabaly app from the App Store or Google Play. Create your account with your phone number, Google, or Apple ID. Complete your profile for access to all marketplace features.

2

Browse & Discover

Explore thousands of verified livestock listings. Use filters to find exactly what you need — by category (cattle, sheep, goats, poultry), location, price range, and health certification status. Each listing includes photos, detailed specs, and often voice descriptions in local languages.

3

Chat & Negotiate

This is what makes Garabaly different. Start a conversation with the seller — ask about the animal, request more photos or videos, and negotiate the price. In West Africa, price is a conversation, not a label. Garabaly respects that. Reach consensus, then move to payment.

"Price is a conversation, not a label. That's not friction — that's culture."

4

Review Seller & Health Info

Check the seller's verification status, ratings, and reviews from previous buyers. Review the animal's health certificate from a verified veterinarian. Know exactly what you're buying before you commit.

5

Add Optional Services & Build Your Bundle

Need a butcher, delivery driver, or vet inspection? Add verified service providers to your order directly in the app. Bundle 1 listing with up to 3 services (1 butcher + 1 vet + 1 delivery max). Drag-to-reorder your fulfillment sequence. Each provider confirms availability before you can checkout — and gets paid immediately when their step is complete. Per-party escrow protection on every part of the order.

6

Pay with Escrow Protection

Once the seller and all service providers have pre-approved your order, tap "Buy with Escrow." Your payment is securely held by Garabaly — one escrow hold, per-party allocation. Pay via Orange Money, Wave, MTN MoMo, Moov Money, bank transfer, or international card. Multi-currency support means diaspora buyers can pay in EUR, USD, GBP, or any of 50+ supported currencies.

"Your money is safe until the cow is in your compound. Period."

7

Track Your Order in Real Time

Since the seller and all service providers have already pre-approved before checkout, your order begins processing immediately. Track it through every stage: confirmed, preparing, in transit, and delivered. Push notifications and in-app updates keep you informed at every step.

8

Receive & Inspect

The seller arranges delivery or you meet at an agreed location. Inspect the livestock to make sure it matches the listing. Take your time — the escrow protection gives you peace of mind.

9

Confirm with Your Unique Code

Every order generates a unique 6-character alphanumeric code — one code per order, even multiparty bundles with seller + butcher + delivery. The code is bcrypt-hashed (cost factor 10) in our database. Case-insensitive entry. Maximum 3 attempts before 30-minute lockout. For escrow orders, the code is hidden from seller and providers until you confirm receipt. For cash orders, the code is visible to all parties after checkout. One confirmation, one code — completes both the order and all service bookings simultaneously.

"One code. Six characters. Instant release. Trust delivered."

For Sellers

How to Sell Livestock on Garabaly

Reach thousands of buyers across West Africa and the diaspora. Get fair prices through real negotiation and receive guaranteed payment.

1

Get Verified

Download the app and create your seller account. Complete ID verification to earn your "Verified Seller" badge. This builds trust with buyers and gives your listings higher visibility.

2

Create Your Listing

Tap the "+" button to create a listing. Add multiple photos or videos of your livestock, fill in details (breed, age, weight, price), and optionally record a voice description in your language. Upload a health certificate if available.

3

Negotiate with Buyers

When a buyer is interested, they'll message you directly. Answer their questions, share additional photos or videos, and negotiate the price — just like at the physical market. Garabaly gives you the tools to communicate naturally.

4

Pre-Approved Before Checkout

When a buyer adds your listing to their cart, you'll receive a request to review and accept the order. The buyer can only proceed to payment once you've confirmed. This protects both sides — buyers know the order will be fulfilled, and you know the buyer is committed. No post-checkout acceptance step is needed.

5

Arrange Delivery

Coordinate with the buyer on delivery or pickup. Garabaly connects you with verified delivery drivers right in the app — they're part of the same escrow-protected order, so everyone gets paid.

6

Get Paid Instantly via Confirmation Code

The buyer shares their unique 6-character confirmation code upon receipt. Enter the code and funds are released to your Garabaly wallet instantly. Withdraw to Orange Money, Wave, MTN MoMo, Moov Money, or bank whenever you're ready — it's your money.

The Heart of Garabaly

Per-Party Staged
Escrow Lifecycle

Garabaly's escrow is a world-class financial system with per-party staged release. One escrow hold per order, per-party allocation. Sellers and service providers pre-approve before checkout — no post-checkout confirmation needed. Every transaction is encrypted, audited, and protected by fraud detection.

1. Funded — "Fonds sécurisés"

Buyer pays at checkout. Funds are held in escrow — neither party can touch the money. One payment covers the seller and all service providers, each share tracked separately.

2. Releasing — "Libération en cours"

Buyer confirmed receipt with their 6-character code. Seller's share enters a 24-hour countdown (dispute window). Service providers (butcher, vet, delivery) receive immediate release — no waiting.

3. Released — "Fonds libérés"

24-hour countdown expired with no dispute, or service confirmed. Funds credited to the seller's wallet. Each provider was already paid at their step — per-party staged release means no one waits for the entire chain.

4. Disputed — "Litige"

Buyer opened a dispute during the 24-hour window. All unreleased shares are frozen — already-released service provider shares are unaffected. Dual-admin review required. If resolved in buyer's favor, funds are refunded.

Complete Ecosystem

You control the fulfillment sequence with drag-to-reorder. One bundle = one order ID = one confirmation code. Each provider gets paid as their step is completed — per-party escrow, total protection.

Delivery Drivers

Verified transport professionals who specialize in safe livestock delivery. Track your delivery in real time with live status updates. Escrow-protected — they're only paid after you confirm receipt.

Professional Butchers

Book a verified butcher as part of your order. In multiparty orders, the butcher is activated only after the livestock transaction is complete — ensuring the right animal is processed.

Veterinarians

Request a vet inspection before or after purchase. Veterinarians issue digital health certificates that are attached to listings, giving buyers confidence in the animal's condition.
