How Garabaly Works

A step-by-step guide to buying and selling livestock with negotiation and escrow protection.

For Buyers

How to Buy Livestock on Garabaly

Find the perfect livestock, negotiate directly, pay securely, and receive with confidence — all from your phone, anywhere in the world.

1

Download & Sign Up

Garabaly is in preview via Expo Go while the app is under review for the App Store and Google Play. Install Expo Go, scan the Garabaly code from our Get the App page, and create your account with your phone number — everything carries over automatically when the app launches in the stores.

2

Browse & Discover

Explore thousands of verified livestock listings. Filter by seller, services, price, region, country, featured, promotions, certified, and Eid-ready. Each listing includes photos, detailed specs, and often voice descriptions in local languages.

3

Chat & Negotiate

This is what makes Garabaly different. Start a conversation with the seller — ask about the animal, request more photos or videos, and negotiate the price. In West Africa, price is a conversation, not a label. Garabaly respects that. Reach consensus, then move to payment.

"Price is a conversation, not a label. That's not friction — that's culture."

4

Review Seller & Health Info

Check the seller's verification status, ratings, and reviews from previous buyers. If the seller has attached a health certificate from a veterinarian, review it before you commit. You can also request a vet inspection through Garabaly's service provider network.

5

Add Optional Services & Build Your Bundle

Need a butcher, delivery driver, or vet inspection? Add verified service providers to your order directly in the app. Bundle 1 listing with up to 3 services (1 butcher + 1 vet + 1 delivery max). Drag-to-reorder your fulfillment sequence. Each provider confirms availability before you can checkout — and gets paid immediately when their step is complete. Per-party escrow protection on every part of the order.

6

Pay with Escrow Protection

Once the seller and all service providers have pre-approved your order, tap "Buy with Escrow." Your payment is securely held by Garabaly — one escrow hold, per-party allocation. Pay via card (Visa, Mastercard) powered by Stripe. Multi-currency support means diaspora buyers can pay in EUR, USD, GBP, or any of 135+ Stripe-supported currencies. Mobile money is LIVE via Orange Money, Moov Money, Wave among several others.

"Your money is safe until the cow is in your compound. Period."

7

Track Your Order in Real Time

Since the seller and all service providers have already pre-approved before checkout, your order begins processing immediately. Track it through every stage: confirmed, preparing, in transit, and delivered. Push notifications and in-app updates keep you informed at every step.

8

Receive & Inspect

The seller arranges delivery or you meet at an agreed location. Inspect the livestock to make sure it matches the listing. Take your time — the escrow protection gives you peace of mind.

9

Confirm with Your Unique Code

Every order generates a unique 6-character alphanumeric code — one code per order, even multiparty bundles with seller + butcher + delivery. The code is encrypted with strong security standards and protected by built-in brute-force prevention. For escrow orders, the code is hidden from seller and providers until you confirm receipt. For cash orders, the code is visible to all parties after checkout. One confirmation, one code — completes both the order and all service bookings simultaneously.

"One code. Six characters. Instant release. Trust delivered."

For Sellers

How to Sell Livestock on Garabaly

Reach thousands of buyers across West Africa and the diaspora. Get fair prices through real negotiation and receive secure, escrow-protected payment.

1

Get Verified

Get the app via Expo Go preview (see the Get the App page) and create your seller account. Complete ID verification to earn your "Verified Seller" badge. This builds trust with buyers and gives your listings higher visibility.

2

Create Your Listing

Tap the "+" button to create a listing. Add multiple photos or videos of your livestock, fill in details (breed, age, weight, price), and optionally record a voice description in your language. Upload a health certificate if available.

3

Negotiate with Buyers

When a buyer is interested, they'll message you directly. Answer their questions, share additional photos or videos, and negotiate the price — just like at the physical market. Garabaly gives you the tools to communicate naturally.

4

Pre-Approved Before Checkout

When a buyer adds your listing to their cart, you'll receive a request to review and accept the order. The buyer can only proceed to payment once you've confirmed. This protects both sides — buyers know the order will be fulfilled, and you know the buyer is committed. No post-checkout acceptance step is needed.

5

Arrange Delivery

Coordinate with the buyer on delivery or pickup. Garabaly connects you with verified delivery drivers right in the app — they're part of the same escrow-protected order, so everyone gets paid.

6

Get Paid via Confirmation Code

Upon satisfactory receipt, the buyer enters their unique 6-character confirmation code directly in the app. Once entered, your payment enters a 24-hour buyer protection window. If no dispute is raised, funds are released to your Garabaly Wallet automatically. Service providers are paid immediately — the 24-hour window applies only to sellers. Funds in your wallet can be transferred to other users. External withdrawals via mobile money are LIVE (Orange Money, Moov Money, Wave among several others). Bank withdrawal is also available.
